How It Works

1

Reflection Pause

When you visit a blocked domain, you'll see a 30-second reflection timer asking "Do you really want to visit this website?"

2

Set Intention

If you proceed, you'll be asked to write down your specific intention and choose a time limit (5, 15, 30, or 60 minutes).

3

Mindful Session

Browse with awareness during your allotted time. The extension remembers your intention.

4

Reflection Check

When time expires, you'll reflect on whether you followed your intention and decide if you need more time.
